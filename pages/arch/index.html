Hello, my name is Diogo and I'm an architect. Welcome to my portfolio site. Here I'll showcase some of the projects I've participated since I've graduated in 2012.

I've gathered here an examplar of each type of design I had the pleasure of working on different studios among the many fields in Architecture. From interior design for residences to urban development, passing through all scales of landscape architecture, real estate and heritage retrofit.

My usual tools for designing these projects consists mainly of AutoCAD, SketchUp/3dsMax (where available) and the Adobe Suite. I've also delved a bit in the BIM world (Revit and Archicad) and most recently into the Web Development (if you are interested, check out the other page I've decicated to it.).

Since I'm an "organization-driven" person, I've also started meddling with the Agile methodologies, with more interest in SCRUM. It would be amazing to one day apply those techniques and aproaches to Architecture.
