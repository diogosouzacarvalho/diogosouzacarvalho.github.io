Real Estate Development

  • Studio: STA Arquitetura S.A.
  • Type: Real Estate Development
  • Roles: Designer, Drafter, Presentation
  • Year: 2014

Situated on an area envisioned by Lucio Costa as the future expasion for Rio de Janeiro, where it's new center should develop, the project is composed by four adjoining blocks. In one of those blocks, the first Hilton Hotel in Rio de Janeiro, was developed by the studio together with a future Offices Building. The other three blocks were so divided:

  • Small density residential buildings;
  • High density residential buildings;
  • Corporate towers with mall.
