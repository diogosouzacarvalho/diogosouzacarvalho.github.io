Heritage Building Retrofit

  • Studio: SPE Porto Maravilha Offices S.A.
  • Type: Heritage/Retrofit
  • Roles: Drafter, Legal Approval, Presentation, Construction Details
  • Year: 2016

Originally the main offices/press of a local newspaper in Rio de Janeiro, the building was designed by Oscar Niemeyer and later achieved the status of municipal heritage. The retrofitting aimed to transform the building on a new office building for small to large companies, amidst the local revitalization plans for the Rio de Janeiro's harbor area.

Joined the team of SPE PMO with the goal of drafting the needed drawings for approval within the municipality. Afterwards, integrated the team responsible for developing the details for the already on-going construction.
